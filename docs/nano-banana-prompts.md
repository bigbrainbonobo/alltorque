## Image Requirements Summary

### Technical Specifications
- **Resolution:** Minimum 3840px wide for hero images (2x retina)
- **Aspect Ratios:**
  - Hero: 16:9 or 21:9 (cinematic wide)
  - Section images: 16:9 or 4:3
  - Split sections: 1:1 or 4:5
- **Format:** High-quality output for WebP/AVIF conversion
- **Color Space:** sRGB
- **Optimization Target:** 60-75% compression, <300KB final size

### Visual Direction (from DESIGN.md)
- Cinematic industrial realism
- High contrast, dramatic lighting
- Dark, moody color grading (70-80% saturation)
- Boost orange/amber tones (workshop lights, golden hour)
- Cool shadows with slight blue tint
- Deep blacks, not grey
- Dynamic angles and low perspectives
- Negative space for text overlays
- Professional but authentic (not overly staged)

### Motion Considerations (from motion-plan.md)
- Hero image will zoom 1.0 → 1.05 (allow 10% edge bleed)
- Parallax movement (ensure important elements aren't at extreme edges)
- Dark gradients will be overlaid for text readability
- Images should work with subtle zoom/parallax without breaking composition

## Post-Processing Guidelines

### Color Grading
- Reduce saturation to 70-80%
- Boost orange/amber tones (+10-15%)
- Add cool blue tint to shadows (-5 to -10)
- Increase contrast (+10-15%)
- Deepen blacks (set black point to 5-10)

### Sharpening
- Apply moderate sharpening (amount: 0.5-0.8)
- Use smart sharpening to avoid noise amplification
- Focus on edge details (truck outlines, mechanical details)

### Grain/Texture
- Add subtle film grain (2-3% opacity)
- Use fine grain, not coarse
- Apply after all other adjustments

### Optimization
- Export high-quality JPEG (90-95% quality)
- Convert to WebP (75% quality, ~50% smaller)
- Optional: Convert to AVIF (65% quality, ~70% smaller)
- Provide fallbacks: AVIF → WebP → JPEG
