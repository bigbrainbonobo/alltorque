Our Story

North Geelong Diesel was established to provide premium diesel service to the commercial vehicle industry in Geelong. What started as a small workshop has grown into a fully-equipped modern facility capable of servicing the largest heavy commercial vehicles.

Our commitment to quality service, modern equipment, and ongoing technician training has made us the trusted choice for fleet operators and owner-drivers throughout the region.

Today, we service hundreds of commercial vehicles annually, from individual owner-operators to large fleet management companies. Our reputation is built on reliability, expertise, and honest service.
